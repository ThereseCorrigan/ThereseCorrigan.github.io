Bath & Bathing

When thinking of soaking in the bathtub, I envision gentle music playing in the background; lights dimmed with a candle or more providing a soft glow about the room, maybe a glass of wine, and stepping into the soothing tub water, that is just the right temperature. Pure bliss. You can choose from scented bath oils, bath salts, herbal bath teas, milk-based bath soaks (some with foaming action), bath melts, or bath bombs with gentle fizzing action. My goal with these Bath & Bathing products is to create and enhance your bathing pleasure.

Please Note: We prefer to use glassware with our products verse plastic for several reasons; our primary reasoning is because many essential oils can corrode plastic containers. Secondly, I like to,when able, use novelty glassware and containers. This is especially true regarding the various holidays, seasons, and special occasions. Lastly, our reason for glassware is it just presents a more classic look and feel to our products.

Bath Oils

Bath Oils bases are available in one of the following: Honey, Apple Cider Vinegar or Combinations of Key Oils. The key oil combination will depend on the product chosen. Additional ingredients can include but are not limited to Phthalate Free fragrances, essential oils, Vitamin E oil, vegetable glycerin, and herbal infused oils.
Stock packaging is available in the form of glass jars (4 and 8ounces)
Use 1/8 cup per bath.

Bath Salts

Principal ingredients for Bath Salt products include Epsom salt, sea salt, dendrite salt (helps with fragrance retention), sodium bicarbonate, sodium borate, white kaolin clay. Miscellaneous ingredients will depend on bath salt combination chosen. These ingredients can include but are not limited to Phthalate Free fragrances, essential oils, herbs, flowers, micas and/or herbal infused oils (for colorants).
Stock packaging is available in the form of glass jars (4, 8, and 16 ounces) or paper-lined bags.
Bath Salts are priced at $0.95 per ounce. Use ¼ cup per bath.

Herbal Bath Teas

Principal ingredients for Herbal Bath Teas are a variety of herbs and flowers; which will be related to the properties of each. These properties include but are not limited to aroma, relaxing, smooth skin and tired muscles. Bath Teas may also include Epsom salt, colloid oatmeal, powdered milk products, and custom ordered ingredients. We have a stock of what we call our standard Herbal Bath Teas and will happy to customize.
Herbal Bath Teas are sold in a set of 3 Teabags for $5.00.
Individual Teabags are available at $1.75. One tea bag can be used for several baths.
