Embroidery Items

Embroidery Items, yes, we also create embroidery items. However, be aware that these products are of limited quantities only; as we are not a commercial embroidery company.

Please note that while we pride ourselves on creating beautifully embroidered items, we attempt to make them functionally as well. This is especially true regarding the linen hand (guest) towels, table linens, kitchen tea towels, and hot pads. These items are worthy of display but, also, they are made to be used. We pre-wash said items before embroidering a design, and then after the design is completed we wash the item again; this is to help prevent shrinkage and possible distortion of the design. Each item will include care instructions to help extend the life of the item.
