Second idea, topic, would be a blog page.

The blog page would log the trials and tribulations of owning a "Fixer Upper House”, including “Before and After” pictures, and provide links to helpful websites such as thisoldhouse.com. This Old House website is great, in fact as they say:

Whether you're looking for a step-by-step guide for your next home improvement project or some design inspiration for your upcoming remodel, This Old House has you covered. Get expert advice on home improvement, gardening, decorating and more today!

I have to say, that I now having an understanding of what it entails to create a webpage; I have a great deal of respect for those who design Web pages! The time and attention to detail are incredible. Just one tag or mislabeled element can make a world of difference.
