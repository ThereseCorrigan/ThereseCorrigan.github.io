Soaps

Soaps…are necessary and something you have to use to clean the body. However, at A Bit of Heaven and Earth LLC, we would like you to think of soap differently. We encourage you to enjoy using the soap itself. This form of enjoyment could come from the luscious lathering, the gentle aroma, or the fun shape of the soap that brings a smile to your face.

Types of Soaps Available:

  • Bar Style
  • Novelty Shapes
  • Guest Size
  • Liquid
  • Whipped
