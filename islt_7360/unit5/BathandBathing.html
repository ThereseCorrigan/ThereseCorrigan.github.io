Bath & Bathing

When I think of soaking in the bathtub, I envision gentle music playing in the background; lights dimmed with a candle or more providing a soft glow about the room, maybe a glass of wine, and stepping into the soothing tub water, that is just the right temperature. Pure bliss. You can choose from scented bath oils, bath salts, herbal bath teas, milk-based bath soaks (some with foaming action), bath melts, or bath bombs with gentle fizzing action. My goal with these Bath & Bathing products is to create and enhance your bathing pleasure.

Bath Salts

Principal ingredients for Bath Salt products include Epsom salt, sea salt, dendrite salt (helps with fragrance retention), sodium bicarbonate, sodium borate, white kaolin clay. Miscellaneous ingredients will depend on bath salt combination chosen. These ingredients can include but are not limited to Phthalate Free fragrances, essential oils, herbs, flowers, micas and/or herbal infused oils (for colorants).
Regarding product size availability; I like to use novelty glassware and containers. This is especially true regarding the various holidays, seasons, and special occasions. Stock packaging is available in the form of glass jars (4, 8, and 16 ounces) or paper-lined bags. Bath Salts are priced at $ $0.95 per ounce.
